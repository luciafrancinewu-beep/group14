What is JARVIS?v2

JARVIS (Just Another Rather Very Intelligent System) v2 is the second-generation intelligence triage system. It ingests any uploaded content and produces structured, severity-rated intelligence reports with nanotech precision.

🚦

Severity Rating System

  • 🚨 HIGH

    Immediate Action Required

    Active threat, breach, critical failure. v2 auto-notify.

  • ⚠️ MEDIUM

    Needs Attention

    Potential risk, developing situation. v2 auto-ticket.

  • ℹ️ LOW

    Monitor Only

    Informational, minor concern. v2 trend analysis.

  • ✅ CLEAR

    All Clear

    No concerns. Within expected parameters.

Every report must flag what needs attention — no ambiguity.
