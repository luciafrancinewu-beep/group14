# First Aid Quick Reference

**CPR:** 30 compressions : 2 breaths, 100-120/min, 5-6cm deep
**Choking:** 5 back blows → 5 abdominal thrusts
**Bleeding:** Direct pressure, elevate, bandage
**Burns:** Cool running water 20 min → cover with cling film
**Heat Stroke:** >40°C, confusion, hot dry skin — **CALL 995**
**Recovery Position:** Unconscious + breathing → on side, airway open
